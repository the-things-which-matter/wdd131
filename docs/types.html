Different Video Formats

YouTube Shorts

YouTube Shorts is designed for quick, engaging videos, usually under 60 seconds. The algorithm prioritizes short-form content, making it easier to go viral. To succeed with Shorts, creators should use strong hooks, maintain high retention, and keep viewers engaged throughout.

Long-form YouTube Videos

Long-form videos provide more depth, allowing creators to tell stories, educate, and build stronger connections with audiences. These videos have better watch time, increased revenue potential, and long-term discoverability.
