Monetization

Monetization is when you start earning money from youtube as people enjoy your content.YouTube monetization system enables creators to earn from their content.there are different monetization threshholds in the two different programmes as we will explore them. The youtube partner programe pays creators as it runs ads.

Shorts Monetization

horts monetization allows creators to earn from short-form videos through ads, the YouTube Shorts Fund, and Super Thanks. With billions of daily views, Shorts offers a fast way to grow and start making money. As YouTube expands its monetization features, Shorts creators have more opportunities to profit.To qualify for YouTube Shorts monetization, creators must have at least 1,000 subscribers and 10 million Shorts views in the last 90 days. Meeting these requirements unlocks ad revenue sharing, allowing creators to earn from their short-form content.

Long-form Video Monetization

Once eligible, creators can monetize their long-form videos through YouTube ads (skippable ads, non-skippable ads, display ads, etc.). To qualify for Youtube partternship programe, a channel needs at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours over the last 12 months.
