Monetization

YouTube offers various ways to monetize your content, from ads to memberships. Learn about the eligibility criteria and the different programs available to creators.

Shorts Monetization

YouTube Shorts allows creators to earn revenue from ads, the YouTube Shorts Fund, and Super Thanks. To qualify, creators need 1,000 subscribers and 10 million Shorts views within the last 90 days. Achieving these milestones unlocks ad revenue sharing.

Learn More

Long-form Video Monetization

Monetize your long-form content through YouTube ads once you're eligible for the YouTube Partner Program. You'll need at least 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours over the last 12 months to qualify.
